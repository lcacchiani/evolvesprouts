Design Notes

  • Concept: "Bold Sprout" — a high-contrast, modern split design. The navy-to-orange diagonal on the front creates dramatic visual tension while the bottom rainbow stripe grounds it in the full brand palette.
  • Front: Deep navy (#174879) with a bold diagonal orange panel. The logo and person info sit on the dark side for maximum legibility. A leaf silhouette is subtly embedded inside the orange wedge. Dotted geometric pattern adds texture to the navy area.
  • Back: Clean white with a navy sidebar containing the logo and social icons. Contact info uses rounded peach-colored icon tiles for warmth against the structured layout. The multi-color top bar echoes the front's bottom stripe for continuity.
  • Typography: Urbanist Extra Bold for the name (matching the website's hero treatment), Poppins for brand text, Lato Light for the role — creating a clear typographic hierarchy.
  • Best for: Professional networking, conferences, B2B meetings. This is the most "business-forward" design while still retaining the organic brand character.
