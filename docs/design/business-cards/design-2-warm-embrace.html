Design Notes

  • Concept: "Warm Embrace" — both sides share the brand's signature peach-to-cream gradient, creating a cohesive, enveloping warmth that reflects the nurturing mission.
  • Front: Centered hero layout with a floating white badge containing the sprout icon. Scattered leaf motifs and concentric circle decorations add organic movement. Orange-to-green gradient divider bridges the brand and the person.
  • Back: Two-column layout — contact details with circular white icons on the left, QR code and social links on the right. A bold orange website pill acts as a clear call-to-action. Soft leaf ellipses create a gentle rhythm along the bottom.
  • Personality: More playful and inviting than Design 1 — targets parents who want to feel welcomed into a community.
  • Print consideration: The warm gradient prints beautifully on matte or soft-touch card stock.
