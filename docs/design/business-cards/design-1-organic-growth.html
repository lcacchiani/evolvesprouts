Design Notes

  • Concept: "Organic Growth" — the gradient accent bar on the left edge transitions from brand orange to green, symbolizing the evolve journey from seed to sprout.
  • Front: Generous white space for a clean, professional feel. Logo + name on the left; faint leaf watermark in the upper right adds organic texture without competing.
  • Back: Warm peach gradient background with structured contact details using branded icon tiles. QR code placeholder for easy digital connection.
  • Typography: Poppins for the name and brand, Lato for contact details — matching the website hierarchy.
  • Print-ready details: 3.5" x 2" standard format, 16px border radius (optional for die-cut), CMYK-safe colors.
