   submit) from 2 users; Apr 17 had one user pick 26 dates. Check
   CRM / admin for any resulting lead record; if none, investigate the
   confirm-and-pay step for drop-off causes.

   from 88% → 66% on `/en/services/my-best-auntie-training-course` is
   the single biggest page-level improvement in the account. Double
   down: add structured FAQ, social proof, and clearer "book a free
   consult" CTA to compound this trajectory.
5. **Instrument the Family Consultations path.** Google Ads already
   sent 15 clicks at HK$6.68 CPC to `/en/services/consultations`, but
   GA4 shows **0 conversions** on that page over 30 days. Either add a
   consult-specific conversion event (calendar click, WhatsApp, email)
   or route these clicks to a page with an existing conversion trigger.
6. **Plan the next Meta flight now.** The Easter formula
   (Reel → LPV optimization → female HK targeting → HK$50/day)
   produced HK$1,400 from HK$322. Queue the next event or cohort with
   the same recipe, using the existing "Free Guide Downloads" campaign
   as the scaffold — change objective to lead / LPV and attach a
   creative.
7. **Lean into SEO.** Google organic is now producing more
   conversions (3) than Google paid (0) and has the lowest bounce rate
   of any meaningful source (43%). Push more cornerstone content on
   "helper training Hong Kong" and "Montessori consultation" themes to
