## Key Takeaways

### Positive signals

1. **2nd user reached payment step**: Only the 2nd person to click
   "Confirm & Pay" — both dropped off at the same point (payment form).
   This is actionable friction data.
2. **Course page traffic doubling**: 10 sessions to the course page
   today vs 6 yesterday. Interest is growing.
3. **Meta campaign successful**: 141 engagements at HK$2.42 each from
   HK$341 spend. The POST_ENGAGEMENT optimization was the right call.
4. **Google organic steady growth**: 29 sessions, consistently the
   highest-quality non-social traffic.

### Action items

1. **Investigate payment form friction**: 2 out of 2 users who reached
   "Confirm & Pay" dropped off at the payment modal. Consider: Is the
   form too long? Is the payment instruction unclear? Is the price
   visible before this step?
2. **Plan next Meta campaign**: Current one ends tomorrow. Decide between
   boosting another post (engagement) or running a traffic campaign.
3. **Monitor Google Ads mid-week**: If still <5 impressions by March 26,
   the HK search market may be too small for paid search.
