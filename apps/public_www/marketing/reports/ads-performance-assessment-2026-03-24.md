## Campaign Wrap-Up and What's Next

### What this week proved

1. **Meta engagement ads work** for this audience — HK$347 bought 144
   engagements, 10,947 reach, and brand awareness among HK mothers.
2. **POST_ENGAGEMENT optimization** was the right call — 18x cost
   efficiency vs REACH.
3. **Google Search is not viable** for this niche in HK — 1 impression
   in 8+ days despite approved ads, broader keywords, and automated
   bidding. The search volume simply doesn't exist.
4. **Instagram drives quality website traffic** — 9 referral sessions
   today with engaged behavior.
5. **The payment form is a conversion blocker** — 3/3 users who reached
   "Confirm & Pay" abandoned at the payment modal. This is the #1
   friction point to fix.
6. **SEO is working** — organic search grew 68% in 6 days (19 → 32
   sessions).

### Recommended next actions (priority order)

1. **Fix payment form drop-off** — 3/3 abandonment rate is the most
   urgent conversion issue.
2. **Launch Meta TRAFFIC campaign** — redirect ad spend from engagement
   to website traffic. Same audience, link click optimization.
3. **Keep Google Ads as passive** — costs nothing when idle. Check
   monthly.
4. **Continue Instagram content** — organic Reels + the /links bio
   page are driving real engaged traffic at zero cost.
